Easy Strawberry Topping Recipe

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 pound strawberries, sliced
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Place a medium pot on the stove on medium heat.
  2. Add the strawberries, sugar, and vanilla to the pot and cook for 18-20 minutes or until thickened, stirring frequently.
